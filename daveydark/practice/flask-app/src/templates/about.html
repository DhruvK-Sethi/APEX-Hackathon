How it started

As the meteor impacts devastated the earth, we velociraptors were forced to adapt quickly in order to survive. We knew that our traditional hunting and scavenging methods would no longer be sufficient in the new world. So, we began to use our intelligence and cunning to find new ways to thrive. We formed secret societies, using our speed and agility to sneak into the ruins of human cities and scavenge for resources. We discovered that the humans had left behind vast amounts of technology and resources that we could use to our advantage. We soon realized that we could use our skills and resources to start our own businesses. We began to build trading networks and establish ourselves as powerful businessmen in the new world. We used our speed and agility to quickly adapt to the changing environment and become successful business owners, all while keeping our true identities a secret from the rest of the world.

How it's going

Our special abilities, such as our intelligence and cunning, proved to be invaluable assets in the business world. We were able to outsmart and outmaneuver our competitors, making us the top choice for trading and commerce. We learned how to read and understand financial statements and market trends, allowing us to make smart and profitable investments. Our speed and agility also came in handy, allowing us to quickly respond to changes in the market and adapt to new opportunities. We were able to make quick deals and transactions, always staying ahead of the game. Our physical abilities were also a great advantage. We used our powerful jaws and claws to protect our businesses and assets, ensuring that we were always one step ahead of our rivals. We were able to intimidate our competitors and gain an upper hand in negotiations. Our ability to communicate and work together in packs also gave us an edge, as we were able to coordinate and strategize effectively. We were able to build a strong network of loyal partners and employees, who helped us to expand our businesses and increase our profits.
